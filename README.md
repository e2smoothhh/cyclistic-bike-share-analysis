# Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis
Google Data Analytics Capstone - Case Study 1
Author: Emmanuel Nwawuihe

## Business Task
Analyze 12 months of Cyclistic trip data to identify behavioral differences between casual riders and annual members to inform a marketing strategy that converts casual riders into annual members.

## Tools Used
- Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn)
- SQL (BigQuery)
- Power BI

## Key Findings
- Casual riders average significantly longer ride durations than members
- Members ride consistently on weekdays during commute hours
- Casual ridership spikes on weekends and in summer months
- Top casual station: Streeter Dr and Grand Ave with 25635 rides

## Top 3 Recommendations
1. Launch weekend membership promotions targeting casual riders
2. Deploy digital campaigns at top casual rider stations
3. Introduce a seasonal summer trial membership
